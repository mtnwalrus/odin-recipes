Breakfast Sammy!

A Good Morning Breaky Sammy

The essential is obviously the soft scramble, which we talked about at length earlier this week. This is no McDs breakfast sandwich (although, to be fair, I have been known to eat and love a Sausage Biscuit with Egg in moments of weakness). But no, there are no perfectly formed egg disk here.

A delicious simple arugula or mixed greens salad with vinaigrette would be great here. Also: fruit, juice and/or mimosas, pastries of some kind, and (obviously) lots of coffee.

Ingredients

  • 1 fresh bagel
  • 1 or 2 farm fresh eggs
  • Sharp cheddar cheese
  • Siracha Mayo
  • 1 handful of baby spinach

Steps

  1. Toast Bagel while you heat the frying pan
  2. Fry egg/s to over-easy
  3. Add a slice of cheese and mayo to toasted bagel
  4. When eggs are ready, slide on to the bagel
  5. Pile high with spinach
